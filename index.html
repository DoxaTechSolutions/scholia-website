The 8 Core Tools

Scholia includes eight professional writing tools, all accessible from your Substack editor. Phase 1 UI is complete; Phase 2-4 brings full feature implementation and backend integration.

📑

Symbolic Labeling

Add symbolic labels (Figure 1, Eq. 2, etc.) to document elements for consistent cross-referencing throughout your essay.

📝

Document Settings

Configure document metadata, author information, publication dates, and other essay-level properties in one central location.

📋

Table of Contents

Automatically generate a navigable table of contents based on your essay's heading structure.

🔢

Section Numbering

Automatically number sections and subsections with customizable formatting (1.1, A.1.a, etc.).

📚

Reference Manager

Organize and manage academic references, citations, and links. Insert citations with a single click.

📖

Bibliography Generator

Automatically generate formatted bibliographies in APA, MLA, Chicago, or Harvard citation styles.

💬

Commenting & Annotations

Add editorial comments, notes, and annotations to your draft without changing the published text.

✓

To-Do List

Track writing tasks, research items, and editor notes directly in your Substack sidebar.
